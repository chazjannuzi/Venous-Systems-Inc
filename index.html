Our story: Venous Systems Inc was founded in 1995 as a durable medical equipment provider serving the Cleveland, Ohio marketplace. In 2022, we were acquired by Match One Medical — and since then we've grown from a regional provider into a prominent nationwide supplier of orthopedic and pain management DME, focused on innovative approaches to patient care.
